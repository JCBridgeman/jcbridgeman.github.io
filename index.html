Jacob Cale Bridgeman

About me

Researcher at the Perimeter Institute for Theoretical Physics.

My research interests include interacting many body quantum systems, particularly topological and symmetry protected topological phases and phase transitions. I am also interested in the application of numerical methods based on tensor networks to such systems. Recently, I have primarily been thinking about topological models defined by string diagrams. In particular, we have been studying defects in these models, and learning how to compute properties such as fusion rules for these.
